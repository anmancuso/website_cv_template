About me

Physicist and Developer

"I am a physicist specializing in direct research of dark matter, currently working on the XENONnT experiment. As a photomultiplier tube (PMT) expert, I gained significant experience testing all 120 PMTs in the Neutron Veto detector, of which I currently serve as data manager. In my analysis and simulation work, I focus on the low-level analysis of data (Waveforms and Electronic noise), and have worked extensively on the Monte Carlo simulation of the detector. Specifically, I am developing a waveform simulator to estimate the tagging efficiency of the Neutron Veto. Beyond my professional work, I enjoy play videogames, read manga and watch animes."
